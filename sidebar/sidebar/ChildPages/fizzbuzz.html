FizzBuzz

Get ready to play FizzBuzz! Input the range of numbers you want to play with and press the button to play classic FizzBuzz where 'Fizz' is printed if the number is divisible by three, 'Buzz' is printed if the number is divisible by five, and 'FizzBuzz' id printed if the number is divisible by both three and five. To customise the game there is the option of putting in your own two words to replace 'Fizz', 'Buzz' and 'FizzBuzz'.
